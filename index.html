About Me

I’m Imran Arif, an automation enthusiast who specializes in building powerful, time-saving workflows using n8n and Make.com. I help businesses connect their apps, move data automatically, and replace repetitive manual work with smooth, reliable automation systems. Over the past few years, I’ve worked on projects like integrating CRMs with marketing platforms, automating e-commerce order tracking, syncing data across multiple sheets and databases, and creating real-time notification systems for teams. I love exploring APIs, designing step-by-step workflows, and finding creative ways to solve complex automation challenges. Whether it’s sending leads directly from a Facebook ad to a CRM, turning form submissions into instant reports, or syncing multiple tools together without human intervention — I make it happen with precision. My goal is simple: to help companies save hours of work, reduce errors, and run their operations more efficiently using automation.
